Information You Provide

The app allows users to enter portfolio-related information such as asset names, quantities, prices, and other financial inputs. Users may also choose to import data files for analysis, depending on the features used.

Portfolio data entered into the app is stored locally on the user’s device. Optimization Tools LLC does not store this portfolio data on external servers.

Purchases and Subscriptions

If users purchase a subscription or other in-app offering, payment processing is handled by Apple through the App Store. Optimization Tools LLC does not receive or store full payment card information.

Data Sharing

Optimization Tools LLC does not sell user portfolio data. Portfolio information stored locally on the user’s device is not shared with external servers by the app.
